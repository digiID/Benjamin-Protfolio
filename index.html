About Me

### Meet Benjamin Adam Shoucair: A Budding Coder in Grade 9 Hello! My name is Benjamin, and I am a student in Class 9. This year, I embarked on an exciting journey into the world of coding.

**Why I Love Coding:** I find coding fascinating because it allows me to create anything I can imagine, from games to websites and even apps. It's like solving puzzles and being creative at the same time.

**What I've Learned So Far:** - **Basics of Programming:** I started with learning the basics, like what algorithms are and how to write simple programs using languages like Python and Scratch.

- **Problem-Solving Skills:** Coding has taught me how to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable parts, which has also helped me in other subjects. - **Building Projects:** I have created small projects like a calculator, a basic game, and a simple website. Each project has been a fun challenge and a great way to apply what I've learned.

**My Favorite Project:** One of my favorite projects was creating a game where a character collects coins while avoiding obstacles. It was amazing to see my ideas come to life on the screen.

**Looking Ahead:** I am excited to continue learning more about coding, especially exploring advanced topics like robotics and artificial intelligence. I hope to use my skills to build something that can help others and maybe even create my own startup someday!

Thank you for reading about my journey. I look forward to sharing more of my coding adventures in the future!
